Monkey D. Luffy

Captain of the Thousand Sunny

Skills

Intense strength, speed, and agility. He has durability, enduarnace, and instinct. He has a very high charisma to the point where he was able to convince a pirate hunter to become the first mate of his pirate crew. Throughout the years he has recruited a numerous amount of strong crew members. Consumed the Gum Gum fruit. Has control over his fruit to the point where he has developed 4 forms over fruit. He has the ability to use all three haki's and he has a good grasp of them.

Experience

Captain of the Thousand Sunny. Has defeated multiple big named pirates in various seas. Fought and defeated three warlords of the sea. Broke into Impel Down to save his brother from being executed. Has gone toe-to-toe with Kaido and Big Mom. Has a current bounty of 500,000,000 berries.

Education

Trained with his brothers Ace and Sabo. Learned the pirate life from Red Haired Shanks. Trained for two years under Silvers Rayleigh. Has knowledge of all three haki forms. Has mastery over 2 forms of haki. Awakened his devil fruit.
